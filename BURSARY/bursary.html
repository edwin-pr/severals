About Us

Ng-CDF is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities and improving livelihoods in Kenya We believe that by investing in education, health, and infrastructure, we can create a brighter future for all Kenyans

Since our founding in 2010, Ng-CDF has worked tirelessly to provide critical resources to communities in need. From building schools and hospitals to supporting small businesses and farmers, we are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of Kenyans

At Ng-CDF, we believe that every individual has the right to a better life, and we are committed to making that vision a reality. Join us today and help us build a better Kenya for all.
